Samantha's Top 3 Rated Movies

I will not be taking questions, comments or movie critiques at this time.

The Lord of the Rings Trilogy

Specifically, the extended editions. From the soundtrack to the cinematography, this movie is endlessly stunning.
Great for most ages and endlessly entertaining time and again, it's a great movie for family night, date night, or just to de-stress after a long week.

10/10, would highly recommend.

The Pirates of the Carribean: Curse of the Black Pearl

With a fantastic cast, Johnny Depp always makes me smile in this movie. It's fun and fast-paced. Another great movie for the family, as long as the kiddo's are old enough for the themes and a little violence.

Top Gun: Maverick

I don't know what it was about the second film that I loved more than the first, but I would highly recommend. You'd never know they filmed the movies decades apart. The director did a phenomenal job making it fresh and new, up to date with current times without sacrificing the "Top Gun" vibes.
